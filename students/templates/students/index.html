Mission

At Dana Academy, our mission is to uphold and continually enhance the quality of education by engaging dedicated and skilled educators at every level. We are committed to equipping our students with the knowledge, creativity, and critical skills needed to excel and compete with confidence both regionally and nationally. The school aims to provide an environment that suits students’ needs helping them excel not only academically, but also in character, creativity, thinking skills, and physical fitness. It makes continuous efforts to ensure that students develop into capable and well-rounded individuals. The teaching–learning process is guided by timely and high-quality educational accessibility and support, enabling our students to be both effective and competent.

ERGAMA

Haalli Kenniinsa barnoota mana barumsichaa qulqullina isa eggate akka itti fufu fi hirmaannaa haayyota barnoota sadarkaa sadarkaan jiran fooyyesse kan hirmaachisu ta’u. Adeemsa baruu fi barsiisuu ammayyeessuun, qaqqabinsaa barnootaa qulqullina qabun barattootni bu’aa qabeessa fi gahoo akka ta’an dandessisudha. - Barattootaa barnootaan qofa osoo hin taane xin-sammuu fi hojii kalaqaatiin akkasumas gahumsa qaamaatiin fooyya’a akka ta’an dandessisudha. - Barattootni beekumsa horatanin hojii kalaqaan akkaa sadarkaa naannoo fi biyyooleessaatti gahoo fi dorgomaa akka ta’anii fi manni barumsichaa barattoota kan simu akka ta’uf yeroo yeroodhaan wantoota barbachisoo ta’an guttachu.
